About Us

Our History

Founded in [2001], Annapurna Computer Centre has grown from a small startup to a leading provider of IT education. Our journey began with a simple idea: to make high-quality IT training accessible to everyone.

Our Team

Our team consists of experienced IT professionals and educators dedicated to your success.Mr. Sunil leads our organization with over 20 years of experience in the IT industry.

Our Courses

We offer a wide range of courses in areas such as software development, cybersecurity, data science, and more. Each course is designed to provide hands-on experience and practical skills.

Our Vision

We aim to expand our reach and continue innovating in IT education, helping more students achieve their career aspirations.

Our Services

Course 1: Web Development

Learn the fundamentals of web development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. This course is designed to provide hands-on experience in building modern web applications.

Course 2: Cybersecurity

Gain essential knowledge in cybersecurity practices, network security, and ethical hacking. Our course helps you develop skills to protect systems and data from cyber threats.

Course 3: Data Science

Dive into data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization. This course covers the tools and techniques needed to extract insights from large datasets.

Course 4: Microsoft office Package

Microsoft Office is a comprehensive suite of productivity tools, including Word for documents, Excel for spreadsheets, PowerPoint for presentations, and Outlook for email. Widely used in business and education, it offers powerful features for creating, editing, and managing various types of content, enhancing efficiency and collaboration.

Course 5: Graphic Designing

Graphic design is the art of creating visual content to communicate messages. It combines elements like typography, color, and imagery to design logos, advertisements, websites, and more. Graphic designers use creative and technical skills to produce visually appealing designs that capture attention and convey information effectively.

Course 6: Video Editing

Video editing involves manipulating and rearranging video footage to create a coherent and engaging final product. Editors cut, trim, and combine clips, adjust audio and visual effects, and add transitions or titles. The goal is to produce polished videos that effectively convey a story or message for various media platforms.
